Who We Are

A trend desk, not a workshop.

We're upfront about what we do: we don't run our own ateliers, and we don't manufacture in-house. What we do well is spotting which pieces are actually worth stocking — watching what's selling, what's showing up on the street and on the runway, and what's genuinely well-made — then building a tight, curated range.

The Global Trend Hub was founded in 2025 and is registered in the UK, with a working team based in Mardan, Pakistan, where a lot of the sourcing and supplier relationships happen.

We're building our first collection now, ahead of our launch on Amazon in late 2026.
